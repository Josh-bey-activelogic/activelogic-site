Your custom software starts as an interview where we sit down in a consultation with you and your team. Our goal is to discover what you really need from the software. We've found that every team wants something special, something they can't get from out-of-the-box or cookie-cutter kit software, so we don't start from a kit. We'll get to know your workflow, your bottlenecks, and the tools you wish existed that we can create for you.

Once we are closely familiar with your brand, style, and personality, we are able to construct wireframes to provide the foundation for your new system. These wireframes outline the front-end design that users will experience, as well as the functionalities on the back end that serve the purpose of the overall system. In collaboration with your team, we are able to refine these wireframes to exactly your specifications before writing a single line of code. 

With a finalized design and plan of action, our expert development team is able to move forward with the actual development of your custom software solution. Security, functionality, and accessibility are at the forefront of our developers' minds throughout the creation of our products, ensuring a reliable system to utilize for your organization. 

While our developers consistently test your custom system throughout the development phase, we have a dedicated team of Quality Assurance developers who take over once the project is complete.  Utilizing a variety of different methods, our engineers ensure that all features in the final system function properly before releasing it to you for full-scale use. 

Once the features have been verified and testing has been completed, we want to see how the custom software system performs in your organization’s environment. While minor bugs and errors may still appear, the overall goals of the solution should still be met. These bugs will be corrected in a timely manner as a part of our customer maintenance program, ensuring your systems continue to function properly.

Custom Software Development FAQs

How long does it take to develop a custom software?

A Laravel project typically runs for 3 months or longer depending on how many developers are dedicated to the team, the complexity of the development, and your business goals. Feel free to Contact Us for a free consultation and allow us to better understand your upcoming Laravel project!

How much does custom software cost?

The final budget for your custom solution can be determined after the planning stage. The cost of custom software development depends on many factors, including the size of your project, the hourly rate of custom software developers, functional requirements, and more. If you share your budget for the development project, we can give you an understanding of what functionality you can get for it and vice versa.

Do you provide software development consulting services?

Yes, our team offers extensive custom software development consulting services. We can help your in-house IT team develop new software solutions and figure out custom software business needs. If you are planning to build custom software and need an external technical opinion, we are here to help.

Do you hire offshore software developers?

Many business owners are thinking about outsourcing software development to offshore companies. These developers have lower prices that may seem appealing. In reality, such a decision could cost you a significant amount of money, time, and effort. In the end, you get a product that may not have adequate support, security, and functionality.

At Active Logic, we don’t work with offshore developers. Our team is located nationwide from Hawaii to Florida, allowing for all time zones to be covered by our team of software engineers. That’s why we are always ready to develop, maintain, and support your custom app.

How secure is custom software development?

With the constant threat of brute force attacks, malware, and ransomware, it’s more important than ever to ensure your web application incorporates strong security measures. Unfortunately, core PHP doesn’t contain any native security measures — they all need to be layered in separately and customized to work.

Why do software development projects often fall behind the schedule and shoot over the budget?

Custom software development involves a huge variety of factors that often change in the process. That’s why the timeline and budget are rarely exact. To avoid these issues, it’s essential to work closely with the custom software development company at all stages of the project. The more your team communicates with ours, the easier it will be to give you an exact estimate and stick to the timeline and budget.
